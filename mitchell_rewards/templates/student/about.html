About Mitchell Rewards

Welcome to Your Rewards Journey!

The Mitchell Rewards program is designed to recognize and celebrate your positive contributions to our school community. Every point you earn is a step towards exciting rewards!

This page is your guide to understanding how the system works and how you can make the most of it.

How to Earn Points:

Achieve high marks, show significant improvement, or demonstrate exceptional effort in your studies.

Exhibit respect, kindness, and responsibility towards peers and staff.

Participate in school events, clubs, volunteer activities, or help maintain a positive school environment.

Take initiative, lead by example, and contribute to group projects or school initiatives.

Show dedication to learning, overcome challenges, and consistently strive to do your best.

Teachers can award points for specific actions or behaviors they observe that align with school values.

Keep an eye out for announcements from your teachers and school administration for special point-earning opportunities!

What Can You Do with Points?

Your earned points can be used to purchase a variety of exciting items and experiences from the Item Shop. From stationery and vouchers to unique school experiences, there's something for everyone!

Visit the Shop page to see the latest available rewards and start saving up for your favorites!

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the Mitchell Rewards program, your points balance, or how to redeem rewards, please don't hesitate to speak to your teacher or a school administrator.

We're here to help you succeed and make your time at Mitchell High School even more rewarding!
